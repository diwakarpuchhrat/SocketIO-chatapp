Features

Streamline is a web app where you can join rooms, send messages and chat with your friends. You will also be able to send and share images and possibly files too over a secure network.

Here is how it works

Upon login, Create a room.

Or if you have a Room Id you can join a room.

And that's it

Text messages are not recorded anywhere and are destroyed upon logging out, hence your privacy is protected.
